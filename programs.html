Food Security Program

Ensuring no family in our community goes hungry

Nourishing Our Community

Our Food Security Program addresses hunger and malnutrition in Nellmapius and surrounding areas through comprehensive food distribution initiatives.

Monthly Food Parcels

We provide nutritious food parcels to 500+ vulnerable families each month, containing staples like maize meal, rice, beans, and vegetables.

After-School Feeding Scheme

Hot meals served daily to 200+ children after school, ensuring they have the nutrition needed to focus on their studies.

Community Gardens

We've established 5 community gardens where residents learn sustainable farming techniques and grow fresh produce.

Education Support Program

Empowering the next generation through education

Investing in Futures

Our Education Support Program removes barriers to learning and provides resources for academic success to children from disadvantaged backgrounds.

School Supplies Distribution

We provide uniforms, stationery, textbooks, and backpacks to 120+ students at the beginning of each school year.

After-School Tutoring

Daily tutoring sessions in mathematics, English, and science for students from grades R-12, led by qualified volunteer educators.

Bursary Program

Financial assistance for promising students to pursue tertiary education, with 15 students currently supported in universities and colleges.

Creating safe and dignified living spaces

Accessibility Modifications

Installing ramps, grab bars, and other modifications for elderly and disabled residents.

Weatherization

Improving insulation, sealing drafts, and other measures to make homes more energy efficient.
